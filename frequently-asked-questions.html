FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What are the basic rules for subscribing to WEPA!BOX? #1

Following are the instructions for subscribing to our service:

1. Go to www.wepabox.com and click on the "Subscribe now" button.
2. Choose a plan: monthly, annual or gift.
3. Complete the subscription form, including billing and shipping addresses. Here you can choose to send it to an address other than yours.
4. Verify your purchase and pay.

Remember that the boxes are sent by mail on the 20th of each month. If there is a change, it will be announced through social networks and/or by email. You can subscribe at any moment, but all subscriptions for the current month must be received before the 18th of the month. Subscriptions received after that day will receive the box assigned for the next month. All subscriptions are renewed and are charged automatically on the first day of each month. The subscriber may cancel the Monthly Subscription at any time by entering wepabox.com using the username and password and following the instructions for cancelling a subscription before the 1st day of each month. Otherwise WEPA!BOX will proceed to charge for the Subscription applicable to the following month. If you have any questions about your Subscription, you can write to clientservice@wepabox.com and we will gladly provide you with help.

How to check the mailing status? #2

To check your box’s mailing status, you may use the tracking number that was sent to you via e-mail at the time of shipping. If your box hasn’t arrived by the assigned date, contact us so we may solve it expeditiously.

How to sign up? #3

Please enter: www.wepabox.com and click on "JOIN NOW".

Enter your personal information in “MY ACCOUNT”. Please make sure all the blanks are correctly filled out

Click “SELECT”.

Select the subscription option you would like in “SUBSCRIBE”.

Verify the information and verify the total amount to be charged. If you are unsure whether the data entered is correct, you can navigate to the previous page by using the return button of your browser.

Enter “PROCEED TO CHECKOUT”.

Enter your payment details in “BILLING DETAILS”. Confirm your order in

“PROCEED TO PAYMENT”.

After confirming the payment, your order has been made.

You will automatically be redirected to your user profile where you can change and update your personal data.

If you have any doubts or questions, contact our customer service department.

How to include a product in a WEPA!BOX? #4

We are always looking for new and innovative products for our subscribers. Please send us an email to info@wepabox.com with all the information and photos of your product, or contact us through the Contact form, and we will get back to you.

What is inside a WEPA!BOX? #5

Inside each WEPA!BOX, is a variety of exciting products made in Puerto Rico. The items in the box will be a surprise to all subscribers; you will always find something new!

Is a subscription necessary to receive a WEPA!BOX? #6

Yes, but you can unsubscribe at any time.

Is it possible to order multiple WEPA!BOXES throughout the month? #8

Yes. Multiple subscriptions may be made by using the details of the intended recipients in the subscription page.

Once the first WEPA!BOX has arrived, what’s next? #9

Enjoy! Try the products and let us know what you think, we want to hear your input. If you are satisfied with your box, continue with your subscription. If not, cancel your subscription before the 15th day of the month by contacting us through the Contact form.

What to do if a product arrives in poor condition? #10

You may reach us by using the Contact form, and we will send you a new product in your next box.

What forms of payment are accepted? #11

We accept payments made with credit/debit cards via Visa, Mastercard, and/or American Express, or Pay Pal.

How to update credit/debit card information? #12

Login into your user profile via the “My Account” button at the top of the website. From there, all personal information may be updated.

Is there a credit card authorization procedure? #13

Upon registering, restarting regular deliveries, reactivating your account or changing credit card details, a small payment might be charged to your card in order to receive authorization from the credit card company and to prevent fraud. This charge will be immediately credited back to your account. Some banks may take longer reflecting this charge in your account balance.

Can WEPA!BOX be sent as a gift? #14

Yes! Click on the “Give as a Gift” button in the main page of our website and fill out the form that follows.

Can a WEPA!BOX be returned in exchange for a refund? #15

For safety reasons, we are unable to accept any returns once the box has left our warehouse. If you are unsatisfied with your box for any reason, you may reach us by using the Contact form, and we will do our best to resolve the issue.
